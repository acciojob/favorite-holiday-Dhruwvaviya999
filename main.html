GOA

Goa is a small state on India's southwestern coast, renowned for its beautiful beaches, Portuguese colonial architecture, vibrant culture, and cuisine, largely influenced by its long period of Portuguese rule; it's a popular tourist destination known for its laid-back atmosphere and festive spirit.

  1. Fort Aguada: A 17th century fort on the shores of Sinquerim Beach
  2. Dudhsagar Falls: A natural spectacle in the Western Ghats with a cascade of milky white waters
  3. Calangute Beach: The largest beach in north Goa, known for water sports like parasailing and jet skiing
  4. Old Goa: A place to explore the culture of Goa
  5. Panjim (Panaji): A place to explore the city of Goa
  • Prawn balchao: A spicy, tangy pickle made with prawns, dried red chilies, and spices
  • Chicken cafreal: A flavorful, green dish made with chicken, herbs, and spices
